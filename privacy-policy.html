This app requires permissions to:

  • View your YouTube videos and playlists - needed to load your playlists into the app
  • View your YouTube profile - needed to identify which account is active
  • Store configuration data in your Google Drive - needed to sync your settings across devices
 

Information collected

This app does not collect any personal information. It caches playlist metadata (such as playlist IDs and titles) locally on your device and in your Google Drive so your data stays synced. No data is transmitted to any external servers.

 

Data retention

Cached data remains on your device or in your Google Drive until you remove it or uninstall the app. To remove the data from Google Drive, open Settings -> Manage apps, find this app in the list, and choose the removal option you prefer.

 

Use of information

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
